Mission & Vision

BDF - Bashorun Descendants Foundation, Africa

Established in 2022 as a Massachusetts nonprofit corporation, Bashorun Descendants Foundation Inc promotes education and empowers American, Nigerian, and Malaysian youths through the provision of educational materials and resources.

Such resources include, but are not limited to, educational books, educational magazines, computers, computer peripherals, software, and learning tools.

BDF MISSION

Our Mission is to build a strong Infrastructural support with community initiatives in rural Sub Sahara Africa

BDF VISION

Bashorun Descendant’s Foundation promotes educational development in Africa by distributing educational resources to those in need, while providing access to educational facilities and other necessary infrastructure.Since 2010, Bashorun Descendants Foundation has worked directly with grassroots Nigerian and African NGOs and accredited learning institutions to distribute school supplies such as workbooks, pencils and sports equipment to schools in need.
