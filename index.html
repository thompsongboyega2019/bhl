Welcome To Bashorun Descendants Foundation, Africa

Established in 2022 as a Massachusetts nonprofit corporation, Bashorun Descendants Foundation Inc promotes education and empowers American, Nigerian, and Malaysian youths through the provision of educational materials and resources.

Such resources include, but are not limited to, educational books, educational magazines, computers, computer peripherals, software, and learning tools.

What We Do AT BDF, Africa

At BDF Africa, our mission is to build robust infrastructural support in rural Sub-Saharan Africa through our diverse programs and activities. We are dedicated to making a positive impact in the lives of families and communities across the region.

Education Support Scheme

Every student deserves a high-quality school. At the Bashorun Descendants Foundation Inc, we believe high-quality schools are where every child is known, challenged, and supported.

How To Help At BHL

At BHL Ventures Group, we see our role as a nation-builder on various continents as an incredible opportunity to lead businesses toward sustainability. Sustainability encompasses not only the conservation of our environment, but also a responsibility to every stakeholder—not only our shareholders, employees, and customers, but also the communities we impact and the suppliers we source from.

Business Objectives

To help our customers to be competitive through superior service, market expertise, logistics and our core capabilities.

Quality Assurance

Through continual improvement of our business processes to always exceed standards set by customers.
